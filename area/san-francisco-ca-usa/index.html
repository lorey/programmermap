San Francisco, CA, USA

This is a map of programmers, developers, and IT influencers in San Francisco, CA, USA and nearby areas along with detailed insights and statistics about projects, languages, etc.

San Francisco is one of the emerging tech capitals of the world. While the tech hotspot of the last years has been the Silicon Valley, the focus of attention has moved northbound to San Francisco. Many big tech companies are here now and hire programmers and developers. Among them startups like Uber, Foursquare, and others. As a side-effect rents are through the roof, but still far cheaper than in NYC.
